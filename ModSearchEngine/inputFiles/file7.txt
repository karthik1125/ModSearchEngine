Cello, It is a string instrument with four strings, It is played by bowing or plucking with strings, which are usually tuned in perfect fifths an octave lower than viola. It is vast member of violin family. It is originated in 17th century in Italy. Its price ranges from about Rs.17000 to 200000. In general it is catogorized under high price instrument. It is generally used as a solo instrument as well as in chamber music ensembles, orchestras.
