Electric Guitar, also called as solid body Guitar is a string instrument which is either fingered, picked or strummed. it uses one or more pickups to convert the vibration of its strings into electric signals. It is originated in 20th century in America. Its price ranges from about Rs.2100 to 200000. In general it is catogorized under high price instrument as a good quality ELectric Guitar will be within 8000 to 20000. It is a very commonly used instrument, it can be played in all occasions and suits all types of music, typically rock music.It was originated in 20th century.
